World's Best Oreo cake Recipe

Ingridents needed

  • Oreo 5packets

    milk about 250ml

    baking soda

    sugar

    • Steps to make

    Step 1 crush oreos into powder

    step 2 mix the crushed oreo with milk to form a smooth batter

    step 3 now make a mold ready for baking by placing a baking sheet on the bottom

    step 4 now mix a pinch of baking soda

    step 5 pour the batter in the mold and microvave for 3 minutes after preheating the oven

    Step 6 demold the cake into a plate and serve hot
